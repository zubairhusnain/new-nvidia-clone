    Immersive Visualization and Collaboration for Enterprises

    Extended reality (XR) and spatial computing are revolutionizing professional workflows with technology that makes it possible to work at scale. Whether holding virtual models in your hands, walking through entire virtual buildings, or rehearsing complex surgical procedures in virtual environments, these technologies are completely changing the ways to interact with immersive data.

    Explore Our Extended Reality Technologies

    NVIDIA's XR technologies include NVIDIA RTX™ GPUs, groundbreaking XR tools, and development software driving the world's greatest immersive experiences.

    NVIDIA CloudXR

    NVIDIA CloudXR™, a groundbreaking innovation built on NVIDIA RTX technology, delivers virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) across 5G and Wi-Fi networks.

    NVIDIA VRWorks

    NVIDIA VRWorks™ enables a new level of presence by bringing physically realistic visuals, sound, touch interactions, and simulated environments to virtual reality.

    NVIDIA VR Ready

    The NVIDIA VR Ready program ensures system components—GPU, CPU, HMD, and drivers—will provide the best immersive virtual reality experiences.

    Katana

    NVIDIA Omniverse

    NVIDIA Omniverse™ libraries and microservices allow developers to build industrial digital twins and robotics simulation applications.

    NVIDIA Virtual Reality Capture and Replay (VCR)

    NVIDIA VCR enables developers and users to accurately capture and replay VR sessions for performance testing, scene troubleshooting, and more.
