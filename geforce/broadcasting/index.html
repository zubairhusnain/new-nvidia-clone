    Your GPU Does It All

    Meet the NVIDIA Encoder (NVENC). NVENC is an independent section of your GeForce® GPU used to encode video, lifting the strain from your CPU. This frees up the system to run your games and tackle other resource-intensive tasks so you can focus on what’s truly important: delivering a show-stopping broadcast.
